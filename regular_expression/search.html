Shield potion

Shield Potion (or referred to the Big Pot by the community) is a Rare consumable healing item in Battle Royale, which can be used to increase the player's current shield by 50 Shield points, up to a maximum of 100 Shield points after consuming two potions. Shield Potions, despite falling under the rarity Rare, can be found relatively frequently. They can be found in Chests, Supply Llamas, Supply Drops, Vending Machines and Ammo Boxes. The weaker counterpart to a Shield Potion, the Small Shield Potion, is more common than the full-size Shield Potion.
